About Us

Coinpliance

Coinpliance's mission is to aid and contribute to the growing Bitcoin industry ecosystem. One of the major hurdles facing nascent Bitcoin companies is the regulatory and compliance requirements at all levels of government on an international basis. Coinpliance seeks to automate and offload these burdens and enable the companies to concentrate on their core business. Coinpliance's guiding tagline is to work towards building, "compliance in a box" for Bitcoin commerce.

Our MOAT

The World's First Bitcoin specific Onboarding Solution

The MOAT acts as a method of protection between a Bitcoin processing company and sanctioned entities and fraudsters. Bitcoin companies can use the Moat for both consumers and merchants in their onboarding process.
